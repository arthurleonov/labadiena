Where are the garments produced?

All our products and are produced in Lithuania. Only the finest materials are used and the quality of the products is guaranteed.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We gladly accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, or regular account payments all through Paypal. Having Paypal account is unnecesessary, although if you already have one you may use it.

How does shipping work?

Orders will be shipped the next working day upon receipt. Shipping inside Lithuania is carried out by the Post24. Shipments are distributed through postal terminals. For list of terminals and more information visit www.post24.lt or contact us via email. Shipping outside Lithuania is carried out by the Lithuanian Post.

How does shipping shipping cost?

Shipping in Lithuania is free of charge. For other countries charges depend on your location and are indicated automatically when choosing your country. All orders that are shipped outside of the EU could be subject to import duty and tax. Please contact your local customs office for further details of charges.
